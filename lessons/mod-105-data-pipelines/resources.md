## Books

### Apache Airflow
1. **"Data Pipelines with Apache Airflow"** by Bas Harenslak & Julian de Ruiter
   - Manning Publications, 2021
   - Comprehensive guide to building production data pipelines
   - ISBN: 978-1617296901

### Apache Spark
1. **"Learning Spark, 2nd Edition"** by Jules S. Damji et al.
   - O'Reilly Media, 2020
   - Updated for Spark 3.0
   - ISBN: 978-1492050049

2. **"Spark: The Definitive Guide"** by Bill Chambers & Matei Zaharia
   - O'Reilly Media, 2018
   - Comprehensive reference
   - ISBN: 978-1491912218

3. **"High Performance Spark"** by Holden Karau & Rachel Warren
   - O'Reilly Media, 2017
   - Advanced optimization techniques
   - ISBN: 978-1491943205

### Apache Kafka
1. **"Kafka: The Definitive Guide, 2nd Edition"** by Gwen Shapira et al.
   - O'Reilly Media, 2021
   - Real-time data and stream processing
   - ISBN: 978-1492043089

2. **"Kafka Streams in Action"** by Bill Bejeck
   - Manning Publications, 2018
   - Stream processing with Kafka
   - ISBN: 978-1617294471

### Data Quality
1. **"Data Quality Fundamentals"** by Barr Moses et al.
   - O'Reilly Media, 2022
   - Data observability and quality
   - ISBN: 978-1098112035
